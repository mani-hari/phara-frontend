## Send schedule recap

| Date | Email | Subject | Audience |
|---|---|---|---|
| Tue **May 19**, 9 AM IST | Email 1 | "Once every twelve years." | Engaged customers (last 12 months) |
| Tue **May 26**, 9 AM IST | Email 2 | "7 days. Then a decade." | Engaged customers + warm leads (last 24 months) |
| Mon **June 1**, 9 AM IST | Email 3 | "Tomorrow." | Anyone who saw Email 1 or 2 but didn't purchase |

**Suppress purchasers** between sends — Shopify lets you exclude the "Has placed orders" segment for the relevant SKU. Set this up once when you create Email 1, it'll auto-apply to subsequent sends.

**Throttle the resends.** Don't blast Email 2 to your full list — only people who opened Email 1, plus warm leads who weren't included in Email 1. Email 3 goes to non-buyers from both prior emails.

**Goal benchmark:** Email 3 ("Tomorrow.") with the single-word subject should drive your highest open rate of the entire campaign. Last-minute purchasers are real and worth the spend.
