## Send notes

- **Quiet hours:** Don't send WhatsApp before 8 AM or after 9 PM in the recipient's local time. Shopify SMS auto-respects quiet hours; WhatsApp Business may not — segment your list by region if possible.
- **Frequency cap:** Don't send any single subscriber more than 3 messages across this entire series. Better to send 1 well-timed than 3 ignored.
- **Suppression list:** Once someone purchases, immediately suppress them from the remaining promotional messages. Send WhatsApp 5 (transit-day blessing) only to confirmed buyers.
- **Reply handling:** Each broadcast will trigger replies. Have a templated FAQ response ready: "Bookings open at the link above. Tier 2 (₹4,500) is most popular and includes the personal report. WhatsApp this number for any questions."
- **Compliance (India):** TRAI rules require explicit opt-in for promotional SMS to Indian numbers. Use only your existing customer database, not purchased lists.
- **Compliance (US/EU):** WhatsApp Business marketing requires opt-in. NRI broadcasts should go only to existing customers who have transacted in the last 24 months.
