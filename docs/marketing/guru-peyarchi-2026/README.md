In Vedic astrology, **Cancer is Jupiter's sign of exaltation (Uchcha)** — his single most powerful position. This happens once every ~12 years. The last time was 2014. The next will be 2038.

For five months — June through October 2026 — Jupiter operates at peak strength. Cancer rules home, family, mother, emotional roots, real estate. Jupiter rules wisdom, dharma, prosperity, children, spiritual growth. The combination is rare and considered uniquely auspicious for:

- Conceiving children and family expansion
- Home and property purchases
- Long-term wealth-building decisions
- Academic admissions and higher education
- Marriage finalisation
- Healing from chronic stress, grief, depression
- Spiritual initiation

This is a once-in-a-decade window. The 2025 product copy can stay structurally similar but the **framing must change** from "another annual transit" to "the most powerful Jupiter transit of the decade."
