# Sometimes the puja is obvious, sometimes it is not

If a family already knows the ritual they want for a clear reason such as Ayush Homam for a child’s birthday or Saraswati Puja before exams, they may not need an astrology consultation first. The intention is straightforward.

But when the problem is recurring, layered, or difficult to explain, astrology can prevent guesswork.

## Good reasons to consult first

- Repeated delays in marriage or alliances
- Career obstacles that continue despite effort
- Ongoing financial instability without a clear cause
- Multiple problems happening at once
- Uncertainty about which graha or dosha is actually relevant

In these situations, an astrologer can identify whether the problem appears linked to timing, a difficult transit, a specific dosha, or a broader need for spiritual discipline and lifestyle correction.

## When a direct puja booking is usually fine

- Festival-related devotion
- Family tradition or annual observance
- Gratitude offerings after a positive event
- Temple-specific sevas for blessings and protection

These cases are less about diagnosis and more about devotion or continuity of practice.

## A practical rule for users

If you are choosing between several remedies and feeling uncertain, start with consultation. If the ritual is already obvious and devotional in nature, book the puja directly.

This reduces friction and makes the storefront more useful. It also helps devotees avoid spending on rituals that do not clearly match the real issue they are trying to address.
