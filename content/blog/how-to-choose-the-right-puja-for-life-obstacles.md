# Start with the problem, not the product

Many devotees begin by searching for a ritual name they have heard from friends or relatives. A better approach is to begin with the actual problem. Is the concern about health, protection, marriage, studies, childbirth, finances, or repeated obstacles? The answer narrows the field quickly.

Different pujas carry different traditional associations. When a service explains those associations clearly, booking becomes easier and more meaningful.

## A simple way to think about matching

### Marriage and relationship delays

Devotees commonly look at remedies such as Swayamvara Parvathi Homam, Mangal Dosha Nivaran pujas, or Lakshmi-Narayana oriented prayers when the core concern is delay, compatibility, or repeated blockage in alliance discussions.

### Health and longevity concerns

When the issue is serious health stress, recovery, fear of surgery, or general long-life blessings, rituals such as Maha Mrityunjaya Homam, Ayush Homam, and Dhanvantari-related prayers are often considered.

### Career, education, and concentration

For students, exam periods, or mental clarity, Saraswati-related pujas are common. For new ventures and removal of obstacles, Ganapathi Homam is frequently recommended. When the situation is connected to timing and planetary pressure, astrology plus a remedy may be more useful than choosing a puja blindly.

### Protection and negativity

Sudarshana Homam, Durga-related rituals, and specific temple sevas are often chosen when devotees feel blocked by fear, negativity, envy, or repeated misfortune.

## Use astrology when the issue is not obvious

Sometimes the same surface-level problem can come from different causes. Two people may both be facing career delays, but one may need a remedy connected to Saturn while another may need a remedy tied to Mercury, education, or confidence. In those situations, an astrology consultation is often the more disciplined first step.

## What a trustworthy puja page should tell you

- What the ritual is traditionally recommended for
- What devotee details are required for sankalpam
- Whether prasad is shipped
- Whether temple proof, photos, or videos are provided
- What timeline is realistic

If a product page lacks that information, the user is forced to guess. For a spiritual service storefront, that is a poor experience.

## A final rule that helps

Choose the puja that best matches the root concern and the devotee’s actual need right now. Do not overcomplicate the first booking. A simple, appropriate ritual performed properly with correct sankalpam details is more valuable than chasing a long list of remedies without clarity.
